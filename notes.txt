# Writing pipeline
- Edit pipeline script directly on configure page of a 'pipeline' job type
  Jenkins > Job (pipeline type) > Configure > scroll down > Definition - 'pipeline script'
- Use Pipeline Syntax sandbox on a jenkins machine
  Jenkins > Job (pipeline type) > Configure > scroll down > 'Pipeline syntax'
